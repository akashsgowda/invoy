# Privacy Policy

Invoy stores invoice, client, business profile, settings, and payment details
locally on the user's device.

The app does not require an account and does not send invoice or client data to
an external server by default. Data leaves the device only when the user chooses
to export, back up, print, save, or share it, such as by creating a PDF, sharing
an invoice, or exporting a backup file.

Users are responsible for protecting any exported files, shared invoices, and
device backups they create outside the app.

Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored app data from the device. Users
should export or back up important records before uninstalling.
